What is all this?

Sourcy is a tool to help you track down missing sources for news articles. So many news articles are published without properly referencing the orginal sources

Status

We're currently focusing on articles based around academic research, where you can track down the published paper or (more likely) press release. The plan is to expand the set of tools to cover all sorts of things that might be sources for news stories.
